## 2026-06-12 — Research findings (key facts)

- **Native gap:** Roam has Ctrl-Alt-N/P (next/prev daily note) but they only work on a dated DNP, not the scrolling daily-log view (Roam issue #615). Keyboard-buried.
- **yesterday-tomorrow:** Standard mode = 3 fixed buttons anchored to real today. Perpetual mode steps relative to open DNP but is a mode toggle, no anchor memory, no calendar, not keyboard-first. → Our niche: always-on re-anchoring stepper.
- **Date utils (use, don't hand-roll):** `roamAlphaAPI.util.dateToPageUid/dateToPageTitle/pageTitleToDate`. uid `MM-DD-YYYY`, title `June 12th, 2026`. Detect current via `ui.mainWindow.getOpenPageOrBlockUid()` (null on log view).
- **Breadcrumbs failure is STRUCTURAL:** RoamJS/breadcrumbs injects into `.rm-topbar` inner flex → crowds native controls, breaks on Roam topbar restructures. Fix: dedicated bar as sibling above `.roam-article`, own overflow.
- **No in-app back/forward API** — plugin must maintain its own visited stack.

## 2026-06-12 — Vision & model decisions (user answers)

- **Unifying principle:** DNP is the single navigation ANCHOR — for time (Date Stepper computes prev/next from open DNP) AND space (Trail history resets & roots at each DNP open).
- **Date Stepper model:** arrows render relative to the *currently open* DNP (always-on, re-anchoring each navigation), not fixed to real-today. Each landed DNP shows its own prev/next. Placement (topbar vs on-page) NOT UX-critical — implementer's choice.
- **Keyboard:** NOT important for v1 (clickable arrows suffice). Note minor tension with Vision phrase "не трогая мышь" — treated as aspirational, mouse-clickable controls accepted.
- **Trail model:** dedicated separate row; last N pages with readable titles; dropdown for overflow (older pages). Opening any DNP CLEARS the Trail and that DNP becomes the first/root crumb. "Back" = click an earlier crumb (no separate browser-style back/forward buttons specified).
- **Trail type:** visited-history (browser-style), NOT block-hierarchy ancestor path.

## 2026-06-12 — Feature-gating decisions (4 forks)

- **Date Stepper off-DNP:** HIDDEN on non-DNP pages and the daily-log view (arrows only on a dated DNP). → does not solve native log-view entry; acceptable for personal v1.
- **Trail root with no DNP opened:** = today's DNP (virtual anchor). Trail is always DNP-rooted.
- **Empty/future date step:** open empty page shell, do NOT create page node (no graph writes).
- **Calendar/datepicker:** OUT of v1 — prev/next arrows only. Calendar → roadmap.
